hr, br, sup and sub - text formatting

hr - horizontal rule

Inserts a horizontal rule/line to divide topics like below. No closing tag.

br - line break

Break line, to control the line break. Commonly used in long texts or to format elements.
No closing tag.

sup

Superscript, elevate off a text of the baseline, like an exponent.

a2+b2=c2

Wikipedia citing a font[2].

sub

Subscript, text below the baseline.

C8H10N4O2, also known as "caffeine."

Using sup and sub altogether below:

1/2 + 1/2 = 1
